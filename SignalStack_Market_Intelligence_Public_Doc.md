# SignalStack Market Intelligence

SignalStack is a market intelligence company building products that help traders understand what matters, what is actionable, and what to watch next across global markets.

At its core, SignalStack combines daily conviction, intraday timing, account-aware tradeability, and live system analysis to turn complex market data into clear, useful intelligence. Through an MCP-powered intelligence layer, SignalStack can access live outputs from its own market engines in real time, allowing it to generate intelligence that is grounded in active system signals rather than delayed or generic market commentary.

The goal is simple: help people make better trading decisions through timely, structured, and accessible market insight.

SignalStack is designed to work across multiple asset classes, multiple timeframes, and multiple delivery channels. Its intelligence can power live briefs, watchlists, alerts, account-aware trade guidance, and narrative market commentary for traders, trading communities, and modern financial media.

## What SignalStack delivers

SignalStack Market Intelligence is built to answer a small set of high-value questions:

- What matters in the market right now?
- Which names deserve attention today?
- What is high conviction but not yet ready?
- What is actionable now?
- What changed since the last update?
- What fits a specific account, profile, or constraint set?

These answers are delivered through a combination of structured signals, market context, and human-readable intelligence layers.

## The intelligence engine behind SignalStack

SignalStack is powered by three connected systems:

**Meridian** provides daily conviction, macro context, and higher-timeframe market intelligence.

**S1** provides research, strategy diversity, experimentation, and comparative system intelligence.

**Vanguard** provides intraday timing, execution-aware opportunity analysis, and account-aware actionability.

These systems are connected through a live intelligence layer that allows SignalStack to retrieve shortlists, market context, conviction signals, and account-aware outputs in real time. This MCP-enabled access makes it possible to generate dynamic market intelligence products that reflect what the systems are seeing now, not just what they saw hours ago.

Together, these systems allow SignalStack to move beyond raw scanning and instead produce market intelligence that is timely, structured, and usable.

They need to know:

## Product direction

The first and clearest expression of the company is the **SignalStack Market Intelligence Brief**.

This can take multiple forms depending on the user and channel:
- a morning brief that defines the day’s important names and themes
- an intraday live brief that highlights what is actionable now
- an evening summary that explains what changed and what remains important
- an account-aware trade brief that filters opportunities based on profile, route, and constraints
- a narrative intelligence layer that translates structured market outputs into readable market commentary

This flexibility is one of SignalStack’s core strengths. The same live intelligence engine can support different products, different users, and different levels of sophistication without changing the core system underneath.
